SmartBite is a mobile-friendly website that helps users make healthier food choices by scanning packaged food products. Our goal is to spread awareness about nutrition and help people identify whether a product is safe, average, bad, or worst for their health.

Our Mission

We believe awareness is the first step towards good health. With SmartBite, users can instantly scan, analyze, and understand the nutritional quality of food before consuming it.
